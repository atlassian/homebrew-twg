Quick start

After installation, TWG CLI is authenticated and connected to your AI coding agent through installed skills.

Verify setup

Run:

twg doctor

That's it. Your AI coding agent can now access your Atlassian data across Jira, Confluence, Bitbucket, and more. Describe what you want in plain English and your agent handles the rest.

  • "Summarize the work I did this week"
  • "What are my open Jira issues?"
  • "Create a Confluence page about our Q2 goals"
  • "Show the current sprint for my team"

See Agent examples for more prompts to try.
